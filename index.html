About Me

I am a Robotics & Mechatronics Engineer with a deep passion for building autonomous systems that operate reliably in the real world. My work spans the full engineering stack — from mechanical design and embedded firmware through to high-level AI and perception pipelines.

My research focuses on the intersection of motion planning, sensor fusion, and machine learning, developing robots that can navigate, perceive, and interact with unstructured environments. I believe the most impactful systems are those engineered with equal care at every layer of the stack.

Beyond the lab, I am committed to open collaboration and knowledge sharing. I have contributed to several open-source robotics projects and regularly publish findings to help advance the field. If you are working on something ambitious in robotics or automation, I would love to connect.
