Domain Randomisation Strategies for Sim-to-Real Transfer in Robotic Grasping

A. Surname, B. Coauthor, C. Coauthor

IEEE Transactions on Robotics, Vol. 40, Issue 3, pp. 1234–1248

CONFERENCE 2023

Hybrid A*/RRT* Planning with Learned Heuristics for Warehouse Autonomous Navigation

A. Surname, D. Coauthor

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA 2023), London, UK

We propose a motion planning algorithm combining informed sampling from RRT* with a neural heuristic trained to approximate the cost-to-go in cluttered warehouse environments. Our method reduces planning time by 42% compared to standard RRT* while maintaining completeness guarantees within dynamic obstacle fields.
